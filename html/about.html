Our School

Located near the heart of Toronto at 851 Mt Pleasant Rd, is where this all started: Northern Secondary School, or as we like to call it NSS. Northern is a large school with almost 18 hundred students as it was founded at September 1930. There are many streams that NSS offers such as the SHSM Program, gifted, technology and business streams.

Our Team

Our team is dedicated to achieving our goals for the First Robotics Competition. Our sub-teams are groups of students which focus on different aspects of the robot. They include our Design, Game Plan, Business, Programming, Manufacturing, and Web Design Team.

Design Team

The team in charge of designing the robot using CAD. In our case we used Fusion 360 to design and model various parts and components for the robot.

Manufacturing Team

All the assembling and putting together the robot happens here. This team takes the designs created by the design team and fabricates them into a fully functioning robot.

Programming Team

The programming team codes the “brain“ of the robot. The movement of the robot comes from programming. Their language of choice for this project was Rust since it provides a great balance between performance and control over systems and functionality.

Web Design

Our team is responsible for the web design and photography for the team. We also made the page that you're looking at right now!
